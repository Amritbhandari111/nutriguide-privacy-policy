Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Allows the app to send reminders and meal notifications.
  • VIBRATE: Enables vibration for reminders and notifications.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID: Used by AdMob for ads.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Keeps the device awake for scheduled notifications.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Ensures alarms and reminders re-schedule after restart.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Used to schedule meal and health reminders at exact times.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: Allows reminders to function under battery optimizations.
  • CAMERA: Used locally to scan food barcodes. Images are not stored or shared.
  • INTERNET: Required for ads and API connections.
