All other user interactions remain private and local to your device.

Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Allows the app to send reminders and meal notifications.
  • VIBRATE – Enables vibration for reminders and notifications.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID – Used by AdMob to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • WAKE_LOCK – Keeps the device awake while showing notifications or performing scheduled tasks.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – Ensures alarms and reminders are re-scheduled after device restart.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM – Required to schedule meal and health reminders at exact times.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS – Allows reminders to work reliably even under battery optimizations.
  • CAMERA – Used locally to scan barcodes for adding foods quickly. Images are not stored or shared.
  • INTERNET – Needed for ads and fetching recipe/nutrition data via APIs.
